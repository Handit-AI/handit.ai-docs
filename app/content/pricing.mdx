## FAQ

### How is usage calculated?
- API calls are counted per request
- Compute time is measured in hours
- Storage is calculated in GB

### Can I change plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer refunds within 14 days of purchase if you're not satisfied with our service.

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Credit cards
- Bank transfers
- PayPal
- Purchase orders (Enterprise)
